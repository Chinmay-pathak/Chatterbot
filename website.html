Dr. Mukesh Gupta

Dr. Mukesh Gupta is a Consultant in Obstetrics and Gynaecology, dedicated to the upliftment of women’s health care standards. He is a University topper also awarded by IMA MWSB as the Emerging Star of Medicine. He has created a Module on “Communication Skill to enhance the Quality of Healthcare” and was invited Speaker at National Quality Conclave, Delhi organised by Quality Council Of India. He conducts various Non-Technical Skills (including Communication Skills / Decision Making / Leadership/ Simulation skills) Workshops which has trained thousands of Doctors.

Education & Knowledge Corner

Dr. Mukesh Gupta owns a very famous Youtube Channel with the name “Le’Nest” which has over 2 million views & is widely appreciated.

The goal of having this channel & making videos was to impart knowledge & healthcare education to one & all. There are many people who suffer only because of being unaware of diseases, their symptoms & how to overcome it. A lot of myths & misconceptions prevail around the globe related to Pregnancy Care, New Born Care & Women Health. Dr. Mukesh Gupta is out to break these myths & misconceptions & make each individual independent & confident with all the possible knowledge of health care.
